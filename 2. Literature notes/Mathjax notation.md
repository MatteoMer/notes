# Mathjax notation

## Basics
- Use \$ <> \$ for writing in Mathjax notation in Obsidian
- Use {} for any ambiguous notation: `2 \pmod {53}`
- \$$ <> \$$ for a new line

## Modulo
- `\equiv` for $\equiv$ 
- `\mod` for $a \equiv b \mod c$ 
- `\bmod` for $a \bmod b$
- `\pmod` for $a \equiv b \mod c$

## Equalities
- `\neq` for $\neq$ 

## Fractions
- `\frac {a}{b}` for $\frac {a}{b}$ 

## Exponents
- `a^b` for $a^b$ 
- `a^{b-1}` for $a^{b-1}$ 
- `a_0` for $a_0$
## Misc
- `\pm` for $\pm$
